Miss call Services

Introducing the innovative Missed Call Alert service through our app, Election Plus aims to streamline communication between candidates and constituents. This unique feature serves as a quick and effortless way for voters to connect with their candidate's office, enabling them to raise fundamental questions about their community. With the simplicity of a miss call and the convenience of a returned call, this service provides voters with a direct line to the candidate's team, fostering an open and inclusive dialogue.

The process is straightforward—voters initiate engagement by giving a miss call to a designated number, using a specific code assigned to the candidate. Shortly after, they receive a call from the candidate's office, creating an opportunity to pose questions and convey concerns. This method ensures that constituents can interact with the candidate's team in a relaxed and pressure-free manner, at no cost to them. The Missed Call Alert service is designed to facilitate a two-way communication channel, making the electoral process more accessible and participatory for all citizens.

Election Plus believes that empowering voters to ask questions and voice concerns directly contributes to a healthier democratic discourse. By leveraging technology to simplify and democratize communication, the Missed Call Alert service reinforces the commitment to transparency, accessibility, and citizen engagement in the political process.
